## Design Inspiration

### Color & Layout Patterns — Healthcare/Care Sector

Look for sites that balance:
- Trustworthy (blue/green tones)
- Warm and approachable (soft backgrounds, natural imagery)
- Clear hierarchy (strong headlines, white space)
- Accessible (high contrast, readable fonts)

## UX/UI Pattern References

### Navigation Patterns

- **Persistent sticky header** with logo + phone number + primary CTA
- **Mega menu** for Services dropdown (shows service cards, not just links)
- **Top utility bar** with phone, hours, and quick links

### Homepage Section Patterns

- Hero with headline + subhead + dual CTAs (primary + secondary)
- Trust bar (certifications, years in business, number of clients served)
- Service cards with icon + headline + short description + CTA
- "How It Works" 3-step process section
- Testimonial carousel (Swiper.js or Elementor carousel widget)
- Team section with photo + name + title
- Careers CTA with benefit bullets
- Footer CTA with contact form or phone number

### Accessibility Pattern References

- Skip navigation link (first focusable element)
- Focus visible on all interactive elements
- ARIA labels on icon-only buttons
- Form error messages linked to fields
- Color contrast AA minimum throughout
