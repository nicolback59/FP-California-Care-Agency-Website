## SEO URL Notes

- All URLs lowercase, hyphenated, no underscores
- Permalink structure: `/%postname%/`
- Service pages nested under `/services/`
- Blog posts: `/blog/post-name/`
- No dates in URLs
- Portal pages excluded from XML sitemap
- Portal pages set to `noindex, nofollow`
