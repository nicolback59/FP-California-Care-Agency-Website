## Page-by-Page Breakdown

### HOME

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Purpose** | First impression, audience routing, trust establishment, lead capture |
| **Primary Audience** | All — families, referral sources, job seekers |
| **Key Sections** | Hero + CTA, Mission Statement, Services Overview, Why Choose Us, Testimonials, Regional Center Partners, Careers Callout, Contact/CTA strip, Footer |
| **CTA** | "Get Started" (families) + "Make a Referral" (SCs) + "Join Our Team" (caregivers) — all above the fold or in persistent header |

---

### ABOUT US — Our Story

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Purpose** | Build trust through narrative; establish legitimacy and history |
| **Primary Audience** | Families, individuals, regional center staff |
| **Key Sections** | Founding story, connection to Trinity Change mission, California focus, growth vision |
| **CTA** | "Meet Our Team" / "Learn About Our Services" |

---

### ABOUT US — Our Team

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Purpose** | Humanize the agency; show qualified, caring professionals |
| **Primary Audience** | Families, SCs, potential employees |
| **Key Sections** | Leadership bios with photos, credentials, roles; DSP spotlight stories |
| **CTA** | "Join Our Team" |

---

### ABOUT US — Mission & Values

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Purpose** | Align with person-centered care values expected in DDS ecosystem |
| **Primary Audience** | Families, regional centers |
| **Key Sections** | Mission statement, vision, core values (dignity, inclusion, independence, partnership) |
| **CTA** | "See Our Services" |

---

### ABOUT US — Compliance & Credentials

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Purpose** | Establish vendor credibility; critical for regional center trust |

| **CTA** | "Download Capability Statement" / "Make a Referral" |

| **CTA** | Email form → PDF download |

### CAREERS — Why Work With Us

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|

| **CTA** | "See Open Positions" |

---

### CAREERS — Open Positions

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Purpose** | Display current job openings in an organized, filterable way |
| **Primary Audience** | Job seekers |
| **Key Sections** | Job listings (title, location, type, brief description), apply button per listing |
| **CTA** | "Apply Now" per listing |
| **ACF Note** | Use ACF repeater or custom post type for job listings — easy for admin to update |

### CAREERS — Benefits & Pay

| **CTA** | "Apply Today" |
